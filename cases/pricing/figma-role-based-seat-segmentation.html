Core Insight
  • Figma prices workflow intensity, not headcount.
  • Free access drives adoption.
  • Role boundaries create friction.
Boundary Ambiguity

The ladder is clean. Real work is messy.

The strategic question is when a free participant becomes a monetizable workflow actor.

PM occasionally designs Reviewing turns into creation.
Engineer gives feedback Inspection turns into collaboration.
Designer inspects code Creation crosses into handoff.
Strategic Opportunity

Attack the role friction

Competitors can make the pricing story feel closer to real work by reducing seat classification pressure.
